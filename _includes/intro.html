Hello!

I'm Chaeyeon (Daphne) Lim, a master’s student at the Human-Computer Interaction Systems (HCIS) Lab at Gwangju Institute of Science and Technology (GIST).

My research explores how systems can support children’s engagement, understanding, and behavior in real-world contexts. Taking a child-centered perspective, I design and study interaction approaches that help users make sense of situations, stay engaged, and act appropriately.

My work combines Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Child-Computer Interaction (CCI) and Affective Computing, with a focus on designing systems that respond to user states and provide context-aware support. I aim to develop practical and deployable technologies that meaningfully assist users in everyday situations.
