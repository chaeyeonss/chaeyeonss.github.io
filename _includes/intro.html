Hello!

I'm Chaeyeon (Daphne) Lim, a master’s student in the HCIS Lab. at GIST, Korea.

My research explores how systems can support children’s engagement, understanding, and behavior in real-world contexts. I take a child-centered perspective to design and study interaction approaches that help users make sense of situations, stay engaged, and act accordingly.

My work sits at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Child–Computer Interaction (CCI), and Affective Computing, focusing on designing systems that respond to user states and provide context-aware support. I am aim to develop practical and deployable technologies that meaningfully assist users in everyday situations.
