Hello!

I'm Chaeyeon Lim, a master’s student at the Human-Computer Interaction Systems (HCIS) Lab at Gwangju Institute of Science and Technology (GIST). I explore how interactive systems can support emotional well-being, engagement, and everyday joy—especially for children with diverse needs.

My work combines HCI, HAI, and affective computing with sensing-based interfaces that recognize user states and foster playful, meaningful interaction.

I'm also interested in spatial technologies that quietly enhance quality of life in homes, schools, and everyday spaces. My long-term goal is to design healing-centered, accessible technologies that naturally support rest and connection.
